Course: Artificial Intelligence
Instructor: Dr. Luis Jaimes
Semester: Spring 2026

Course Description:
This course introduces the foundations of Artificial Intelligence, including rational agents, search, knowledge representation, reasoning, planning, uncertainty, and modern LLM-based systems.

Primary Text:
Russell & Norvig, Artificial Intelligence: A Modern Approach (4th Edition)

Course Structure:
- Weekly lectures
- Programming assignments
- One midterm exam
- Final project (LLM-based rational agent)
